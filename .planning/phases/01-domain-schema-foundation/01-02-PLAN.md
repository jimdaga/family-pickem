| T-01-07 | Tampering | Backfill migration | high | mitigate | Use idempotent `get_or_create` and targeted `filter(pool__isnull=True).update(...)`; preserve legacy user fields per D-12. |

| T-01-09 | Denial of Service | Strict legacy constraints | high | mitigate | Keep pool FKs nullable and defer strict legacy uniqueness per D-11/D-22. |
