| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| T-01-11 | Information Disclosure | `MessageBoardPost.family` | critical | mitigate | Add explicit family FK and backfill existing posts to legacy family for later tenant filtering. |
| T-01-12 | Information Disclosure | `MessageBoardComment.family` and `MessageBoardVote.family` | high | mitigate | Store explicit family FK derived from post/comment target to avoid ID-only leakage in later query paths. |
| T-01-12B | Elevation of Privilege | Message-board-only legacy membership mapping | high | mitigate | Create member memberships only for active users referenced by `MessageBoardPost.user`, `MessageBoardComment.user`, or `MessageBoardVote.user`; preserve owner/admin roles and skip inactive or missing users. |
| T-01-13 | Information Disclosure | `SiteBanner.family` | medium | mitigate | Keep nullable family FK so `NULL` remains site-wide and family-specific banners can be filtered later. |
| T-01-14 | Tampering | Homepage backfill migration | medium | mitigate | Use `apps.get_model`, deterministic legacy family slug, and idempotent updates; reverse only nulls the added columns. |
| T-01-15 | Denial of Service | Cross-app migration dependency | medium | mitigate | Depend on `pickem_api.0073_domain_schema_foundation` so `Family` exists before homepage FK creation. |
| T-01-SC | Tampering | npm/pip/cargo installs | high | mitigate | No external packages are installed in Phase 1; package legitimacy audit says none. |
