How we got here

From one spreadsheet to many families.

The original league

Family Pick'em began as a single family pick league run from a spreadsheet. It worked—until keeping every score, pick, and standing current became a job of its own.

Built for busier lives

As life got busier, the spreadsheet needed an upgrade. Several versions of the site followed, starting with a simple experience built for that one family.

A shared platform in 2026

In 2026, Family Pick'em launched multi-family support—giving more families a private home for their own rules, rivalries, and Sunday traditions.

{% elif page == 'terms' %}

Using the service

Use Family Pick'em lawfully, respectfully, and only with an account you are authorized to use. Keep your sign-in credentials private and do not try to interfere with another family or pool.

Private leagues and content

Commissioners and family administrators manage their own memberships, rules, announcements, and moderation. You remain responsible for the picks, posts, comments, and profile information you add.

For friendly competition

Family Pick'em is a recreational pick'em service. It does not take bets, hold funds, or operate as a sportsbook. Any prizes or side arrangements in a private league are the responsibility of that league’s members.

Availability and changes

We work to keep the service available and accurate, but game data, third-party services, and feature availability can change. We may update the service or these terms as it evolves.

{% elif page == 'privacy' %}

Information used to run your league

When you sign in, Family Pick'em uses account information supplied through Google sign-in, such as your name, email address, and profile image. It also stores the picks, posts, comments, profile details, and family membership information needed to operate your pool.

How information is used

Information is used to authenticate you, show your picks and standings, deliver invitations or league emails when enabled, maintain private family spaces, and protect the service. We do not sell personal information.

Service providers and optional AI recaps

Family Pick'em relies on service providers for sign-in, hosting, game data, and email delivery. If a commissioner enables AI recaps, relevant pool results and standings may be sent to the configured AI provider to create that recap. The recap remains a separate, commissioner-controlled pool publication.

Your choices

You can update your profile details and should contact your commissioner for pool membership questions. For site-level privacy questions, use the contact information on the Contact page.
