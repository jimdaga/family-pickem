Family Football Pick League Rules

Patty Donovan, enforcing the rules since 2019

  • 1.A - Wins are awareded 1 point
  • 1.B - Losses do not get any points
  • 1.C - No points awareded for ties
  • 1.D - 2 points awareded for each week won

  • 2.A - Tiebreaker determined by whoever has the closes to the total score (No Price is Right Rules)
  • 2.B - If a tie breaker total score has the potential to end up in a tie, particpants that are in the running will choose most total combined yards of both teams of the monday night game as well.
  • 2.C - Games are locked automatically the second the given game starts

  • 3.A - If a person picks a "Perfect Week", Every participant must pay the winnder $10/each
  • 3.B - $40 Per Person entry fee. Champion is determined by most total points.
  • 3.C - In the event of a tie at the end of the season for a champion, that participant that are tied will continue into the playoffs.
